Where control breaks down

  • Payment certificates submitted late or disputed without a clear audit trail
  • Variations approved informally, tracked inconsistently, or not at all
  • Contract records spread across email, spreadsheets and shared drives
  • Audit and funding acquittals that take weeks to reconstruct

Delivery Systems provides consulting and software to establish structured, audit-ready control — built for AS4000 administration and DRFA-funded programs.
