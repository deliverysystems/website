## What this site covers

The site represents two distinct but related offerings:

1. **Infrastructure consulting services** — contract administration, payment control, variation management and PMO delivery for local government clients in NSW and QLD. Targeted at councils and program managers dealing with AS4000 contracts, DRFA-funded reconstruction, and audit-exposed workflows.

2. **Delivery Systems software product** — an ERPNext-based PMO tool for managing contract payments, variations, and project workflows. The product is the platform that underpins the consulting offer and can be positioned independently as a software solution.

Both offerings share the same core value proposition: structured, audit-ready control over contract payments and variations. The consulting lead validates the product; the product scales the consulting.
