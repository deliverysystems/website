# scope.md — Homepage Build Scope

This file defines the agreed scope for the Delivery Systems homepage (index.html).
Review and update this file before starting any new work session.

## Build type

New build. Empty repository. No existing source file.

## Output

index.html — single self-contained file (HTML + embedded CSS)

Visual tone: professional, restrained, and engineering-credible. Dark navy
or charcoal base with clean white space. No bright startup colours, no
gradients, no stock photo clichés.

## Page sections

### 1. Hero
Lead with client pain, not the product or service. Pain points to surface:
- Fragmented contract administration
- Missed payment deadlines
- Variation disputes
- Audit exposure

Headline and subheading must open on the problem before introducing
Delivery Systems as the solution.

### 2. About
Brief founder section. Content:
- Founder: Juan, senior civil engineer and program manager
- Experience: AS4000 contract administration, DRFA-funded reconstruction,
  PMO delivery for regional Australian local government
- Tone: short, credible, human — not a CV, not a sales pitch

### 3. Services — two tracks, equal weight
The site must represent both offerings clearly:

**Track A — Consulting services**
Contract administration, payment control, variation management and PMO
delivery for local government clients in NSW and QLD. Targeted at councils
and program managers dealing with AS4000 contracts and DRFA-funded
reconstruction programs.

**Track B — Software product**
An ERPNext-based PMO tool for managing contract payments, variations and
project workflows. Can be positioned as a standalone product or as the
platform underpinning the consulting engagement.

### 4. How It Works
Simple process steps showing how a client engages. Should be approachable
and easy to follow — not a detailed methodology.

### 5. Who We Serve
The target audience. Regional local government in NSW and QLD. Program
managers, directors of engineering, infrastructure leads dealing with
publicly funded construction and reconstruction programs.

### 6. Contact / CTA
- Primary CTA: "Request a Payment Process Review"
- Email: mailto:juanc@deliverysystems.com.au
- No placeholder or obfuscated addresses

## Out of scope for this pass

- Pricing figures or pricing structure
- Standalone pages (About, Contact, Case Studies as separate pages)
- Case study detail (placeholder card acceptable if included)
- AI or automation claims
